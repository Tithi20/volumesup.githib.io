About Us

With Volumes Up, you will find it easy to search the music playlist for all mood types. You can access our website from mobiles, tablets and desktops or laptops.

There are millions of songs available on Volumes Up. So, whether you are chilling alone, studying or having a late candle light dinner, we have got your back. Do not worry about the situation you are in. We have songs ready for all kind of moods.

This is a free application, so enjoy.
